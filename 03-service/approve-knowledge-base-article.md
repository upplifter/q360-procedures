# Approve a Knowledge Base Article

## What This Procedure Does

This procedure reviews and publishes Knowledge Base articles that have been submitted for approval. Publishing makes the article visible and searchable to all users with Knowledge Base access.

## Before You Begin

- Articles must be submitted for review with Draft status
- Have permission to approve and publish Knowledge Base content
- Review any organizational guidelines for article quality and format

## Steps

### Step 1: Access the Knowledge Base

Log into the admin console and go to Knowledge Base. The system displays a list of articles. Filter or search for articles with Draft status to locate pending reviews.

### Step 2: Open the Article

Click on the article title to open the full article view. Review the article content, structure, categories, and overall quality.

### Step 3: Review Content for Accuracy

Read the article carefully and verify:
- Content is accurate and complete
- Steps are clear and in logical order
- Technical information is correct
- No conflicting information with other Knowledge Base articles
- Images and attachments are relevant and helpful
- Grammar and formatting are professional

### Step 4: Publish the Article

If the article meets quality standards, click Publish. The system changes the article status to Published and makes it visible to all users. If revisions are needed, you can return the article to the author for edits.

## What Happens Next

The article is now live in the Knowledge Base and searchable by all users with access. The article appears in search results and is categorized according to the categories assigned during creation. Users can access the article both internally within Q360 and externally through the customer portal if configured.

## Common Issues

**Cannot Find Articles Pending Review**
Filter the Knowledge Base list by Status = Draft to display only articles awaiting approval. Check that you have permission to view draft articles. Ensure notifications are working so you receive alerts when articles are submitted.

**Published Article Contains Errors**
If an error is discovered after publication, open the article, click Edit icon (pencil), make corrections, and save. The updates are immediately reflected in the published version.

**Cannot Publish Article**
Verify you have publisher permissions in your user role. Check that the article is in Draft status and ready for approval. Ensure all required fields are completed before publishing.
