# How to Create a Purchase Order in Q360

## Overview
A Purchase Order (PO) is used to formally request goods or services from a vendor.

## Prerequisites
- You must have Purchasing permissions in Q360
- The vendor must already exist in the system
- You need the item/part numbers you intend to order

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Purchasing** → **Purchase Orders** from the main menu.
2. Click **New** (or press Ctrl+N) to create a new PO.
3. In the **Vendor** field, search for and select the vendor name.
4. Set the **Order Date** — this defaults to today's date.
5. Set the **Required By Date** — the date you need the items delivered.
6. In the **Line Items** section, click **Add Line**.
7. Enter the **Part Number** or search by description.
8. Enter the **Quantity** required.
9. Confirm the **Unit Price** — this may auto-populate from the vendor price list.
10. Add a **Description** if needed for clarity.
11. Repeat steps 6–10 for each additional item.
12. Review the **Subtotal**, **Tax**, and **Total** at the bottom.
13. In the **Ship To** field, select the delivery location.
14. Add any **Notes** or **Internal Comments** for the receiving team.
15. Click **Save** to save the PO as a draft.
16. Click **Approve** (or submit for approval if your org requires it) to finalize.
17. To send to the vendor, click **Print** or **Email PO** from the toolbar.

## Tips
- A PO in **Draft** status can still be edited.
- Once **Approved**, changes require a PO amendment.
- Always confirm the vendor's email is correct before clicking Email PO.

## Common Errors
- **Vendor not found**: The vendor must be created in Accounts Payable first.
- **Price mismatch warning**: Check the vendor's current price list or override manually.
- **Cannot approve**: You may lack approval permissions — contact your Q360 admin.
