# Create a Knowledge Base Article

## What This Procedure Does

This procedure creates a new Knowledge Base article that can be used internally by technicians and externally by customers. Articles are submitted as drafts for review and approval before being published.

## Before You Begin

- Prepare your article content and title
- Identify the Knowledge Base categories relevant to the article
- Have content ready for the rich text editor
- Have permission to create Knowledge Base articles

## Steps

### Step 1: Open New Knowledge Base

Go to Knowledge Base > New Knowledge Base. The system opens a blank article form.

### Step 2: Enter Article Title and Content

Fill in the following fields:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| Title | Enter the article title (e.g., "How to Reset Network Password") |
| Content | Use the rich text editor to write the article body including steps, images, and explanations |

Use formatting tools in the editor to add headings, bullets, numbered lists, bold text, and links as appropriate. Attach images or documents that support the article.

### Step 3: Assign Categories

In the Knowledge Base Categories widget, select the categories that classify this article. Use the checkboxes or selection interface to choose from existing categories. Multiple categories can be assigned to improve searchability.

### Step 4: Save as Draft

Click Save Draft. The system saves the article as a draft and displays a confirmation. The article is not yet visible to other users.

### Step 5: Submit for Review

Click Submit For Review. A notification is sent to the Knowledge Base publisher or approver indicating that a new article is pending review.

## What Happens Next

The article enters the review queue with Draft status. The assigned publisher reviews the content for accuracy, completeness, and compliance with organizational standards. Once approved, the publisher will publish the article and it becomes searchable by all users with Knowledge Base access.

## Common Issues

**Cannot Find Knowledge Base Categories Widget**
The categories widget is displayed on the article form. If not visible, ensure you are on the correct article creation form. Categories may be optional depending on your configuration.

**Article Submitted But No Notification Sent**
Verify that a publisher or approver is assigned to receive notifications. Check your notification settings to ensure email notifications are enabled.

**Need to Edit Article After Submission**
If the article has not yet been published, open the article and click Edit. Make your changes and save. The article remains in Draft status until published.
