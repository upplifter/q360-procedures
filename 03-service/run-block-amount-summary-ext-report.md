# Run the Block Amount Summary Ext Report

## What This Procedure Does

The Block Amount Summary Ext Report is a self-service report presented in the External Customer Portal. Customers can view their prepaid block balances across all service contracts, showing the amount sold, amount used, and remaining balance as of their selected end date.

## Before You Begin

- Customer must have an External Portal user account (see SVC-PORTAL-001)
- Customer must be linked to service contracts with block amounts
- Portal access must be enabled and active
- Customer should know their login credentials

## Steps

### Step 1: Log Into the External Portal

Open the external customer portal in a web browser. Enter your Portal User ID and Password. Click Login.

### Step 2: Navigate to Reports

Once logged in, locate the Reports section of the portal. Click Block Amount Summary or a similar reports menu option.

### Step 3: Set the End Date

Select an end date to display block balances as of that date. Use the date picker to choose the specific date for the balance snapshot. This allows customers to view historical balances.

### Step 4: Run the Report

Click View Report or similar button. The system displays the block amount summary in a table format.

### Step 5: Review Your Block Balances

The report displays:

- Contract No
- Site Location
- Contract Title
- Block Balance (Amount Sold minus Amount Used)
- Remaining Days to Expiration (if applicable)

Review your remaining balance to ensure adequate prepaid service coverage. Contact your service provider if balance is low or approaching expiration.

## What Happens Next

The report displays current block balance information as configured in Q360. Customers can reference this report to monitor their prepaid balance and plan for renewal or additional purchases. Contact your service provider through the portal or directly if you need to adjust your prepaid balance.

## Common Issues

**Cannot Log Into Portal**
Verify your Portal User ID and Password are correct. Check that your account has been enabled by the service provider. Contact the service provider if you cannot access the portal.

**No Block Amounts Displayed**
Verify that your contracts include block amounts. Contact the service provider to confirm block amounts are configured on your accounts.

**Balance Seems Incorrect**
Contact your service provider to verify the balance. Request an explanation for recent transactions or usage charges. Ask about the balance calculation methodology.
